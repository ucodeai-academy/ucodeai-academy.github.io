Programs

We will teach kids to program through Scratch, Python, and more. We offer year-round weekly in-person lessons.

Private Coding Classes

For age 10-16.

One-on-one personalized instruction to suit your child's age, skill level, and interests. We ensure that your child learns at a pace that is comfortable and effective for them.

Group Coding Classes

For age 10-16.

Maximum of 3 students per teacher.

Coding encourages creativity, and in a group, kids can brainstorm ideas, share insights, and inspire each other to think outside the box.

Early Child Education

For age 6-9.

Maximum of 3 students per teacher.

Cultivating logical reasoning skills in a child's formative years is essential for their holistic development, as it lays the groundwork for enhancing critical thinking, problem-solving abilities, and intellectual growth.
